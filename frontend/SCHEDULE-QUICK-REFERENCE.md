## 📋 How to Use

### Schedule a Post (Quick Steps)

1. **Set LinkedIn Account**
   - Click dropdown → Select account

2. **Set Date**
   - Click on date field → Calendar opens → Pick date

3. **Set Time**
   - Click on time field → Time picker opens → Choose time

4. **Set Timezone** (if needed)
   - Click on timezone → Select from 14 options

5. **Activate Schedule**
   - Click **✓ Set** button (turns green)
   - Post is now scheduled!

### Pause a Scheduled Post

- Click **⏸ Hold** button (turns amber)
- Post will not publish until you click "Set" again

## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: What if I click the wrong date?
**A:** Just click the date field again and pick the correct one - it updates immediately

### Q: Can I schedule posts at different times?
**A:** Yes! Each post has its own date/time/timezone

### Q: What does "Hold" do?
**A:** It pauses the post - won't publish until you click "Set" again

### Q: How do I change timezone?
**A:** Click on the timezone label (e.g., "ET") → Select new timezone

### Q: Can I schedule in the past?
**A:** No, the system will prevent this or auto-adjust to next valid slot

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Calendar won't open?
- Make sure you're clicking the date field itself
- Try refreshing the page

### Time not updating?
- Wait for the auto-save to complete (should be instant)
- Check your internet connection

### Can't click Set button?
- Ensure date and time are set first
- Check that post is approved

### Status not changing?
- Refresh the page
- Check browser console for errors
